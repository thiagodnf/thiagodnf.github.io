Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Michigan

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Michigan-Dearborn with Prof. Dr. Marouane Kessentini. My researches mainly focus on the use of Preference and Search Based Software Engineering applied to Software Testing and Software Refactoring activities. I received my PhD Degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Parana, and my Master’s Degree and bachelor in Computer Science from Ceara State University.
